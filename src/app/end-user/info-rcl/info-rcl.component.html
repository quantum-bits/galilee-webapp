What is the Revised Common Lectionary (RCL)?

The RCL is a Bible reading plan used by many Christians across the world. It follows a three year cycle that is connected to the church calendar. Each Sunday there are four readings taken from the Old Testament or the book of Acts, the Psalms, the Epistles or Revelation and from one of the Gospels. On weekdays passages vary from this format.
