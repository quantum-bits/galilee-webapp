How do I use the forum?

Welcome to the Forum feature of Galilee. Research tells us that one of the most powerful means of growing spiritually is to communicate with others about Scripture. The Forum feature is a tool to communicate with others through Galilee. Your group might consist of a small group Bible study, a friend or two, a family or a mentor and mentee – whomever you'd like. On the Forum you can share prayer requests, your reflections from the readings, questions, encouragement, links to other websites – whatever you'd like. Whatever you write within the Forum is only visible to the people you have added to your Forum group. To use the Forum feature, you must create your own personal account on the website. To get started, click on the Forum link at the top of this page. To start a group, or start a group, or add members to your group, please contact the site administrator at galilee@taylor.edu.
