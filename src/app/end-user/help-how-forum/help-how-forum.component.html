How can I use Galilee with my small group?

You can use facebook in conjunction with Galilee to communicate with your small group. To do this, have your small group leader set up a facebook group and invite the other members to the group. Then, if you click on a facebook icon on Galilee, you will be able to post comments to your small group or friends on facebook. Your post will contain a link back to the Galilee page that you were on.
